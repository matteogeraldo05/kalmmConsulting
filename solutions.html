What We Do

Project Management & Cost Estimation

Successful infrastructure delivery starts with clear planning and reliable numbers. At KALMM, we provide project management and cost estimation services that keep projects on track and within budget. We offer support in scope definition, schedule development, procurement strategy, tender preparation, and construction oversight. Our cost estimation services leverage unit pricing databases and real-world experience to deliver Class D through Class A estimates. Whether managing multidisciplinary teams or liaising with municipalities, we ensure seamless coordination from kickoff to close-out.

Land / Site Development

We support landowners, developers, and municipalities through every stage of land and site development, from concept to construction. Our team ensures that civil site plans meet zoning, grading, drainage, and servicing requirements across residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We integrate stormwater management, erosion and sediment control, and utility coordination to deliver fully engineered site development packages ready for municipal approval and tender. By combining technical accuracy with practical buildability, we help clients unlock the full potential of their sites.

Land / Site Development

At KALMM Consulting, stormwater is more than runoff—it's a design challenge and an opportunity. We specialize in comprehensive Stormwater Management (SWM) studies and system design that align with regulatory standards and future-focused resilience goals. Our services include hydrologic and hydraulic modeling using industry sandard modelling software, flood risk assessment, Low Impact Development (LID) strategies, and Best Management Practices (BMPs) for stormwater quantity and quality control. Whether it’s new development, infill, or retrofit, we deliver solutions that manage peak flows, reduce erosion, recharge groundwater, and protect receiving watercourses.

Municipal Servicing Design

Our expertise in municipal servicing design covers watermain, sanitary sewer, and storm sewer infrastructure for both public capital works and private developments. We design servicing networks that meet current and future demands while optimizing for constructability, maintenance, and cost-efficiency. From local collector roads to regional trunk systems, our team navigates utility conflicts, elevation constraints, and permitting requirements with precision. Whether working on greenfield expansions or urban intensification projects, we ensure servicing aligns with master plans, engineering standards, and community needs.
